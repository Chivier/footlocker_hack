    Take Flight
    The Men’s Jordan Retro 5 is the retro edition of Michael Jordan’s fifth signature basketball shoe. The Air Jordan 5 is no doubt one of the most iconic Air Jordan models of all time, ranking high on the list of favorite designs for many Jordan collectors. Originally released in 1990, the Jordan 5 was the third Air Jordan designed by now-legendary Nike designer Tinker Hatfield. The design takes inspiration from World War II fighter planes, which often featured paint jobs of sharks baring their teeth. The shark tooth motif is interpreted on the midsole of the shoe, while the entire silhouette seems to be in motion and ready to strike. The Jordan 5 also introduced two features that would later become signature design elements of various Air Jordans to follow: a toggle lace lock and clear rubber on the outsole. It all adds up to become another masterpiece by Hatfield, and one of the most essential Air Jordans for any sneaker collector.

    Always Iconic
    Whether you’re talking about a classic original colorway like “Black/Metallic” or “Fire Red,” or an all-new look for the shoe designed by today’s Jordan Brand team, there are few sneakers you can put on your feet that are more stylish and iconic than the Air Jordan 5. Here at Foot Locker we carry all the latest must-have releases for the Jordan 5 in a full range of men’s sizing. But like with all Air Jordan Retros, you won’t want to hesitate on picking up your pair, because the quantities of sneakers this iconic never last long. Grab the essential Air Jordan 5 today.

    Men’s Jordan Retro 5 features:
    • Leather, suede, or nubuck upper for most styles. See each style for specific materials.
    • Signature oversized reflective tongue.
    • Clear TPU mesh panels on each side of the midfoot.
    • Toggle lace lock to keep your laces secured.
    • “Shark tooth” midsole detailing inspired by WWII fighter planes.
    • Visible Air unit in heel.
    • Rubber outsole with clear portions for another signature detail of the Jordan 5.
